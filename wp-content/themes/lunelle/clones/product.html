    FAQs

    Can I wear lunelle Deluxe all night?

    Yes of course! Lunelle Deluxe is designed with breathable fabric and extremely soft speakers so you can wear it all night, without the pain of traditional earphones. You won't feel the speakers, even when sleeping on your side!
    I'm a side sleeper - is it suitable for me?

    Yes! The speakers in our Lunelle Deluxe model are only 4mm and covered by soft & flexible foam. You won't feel them at all, even when you’re on your side.

    Will if fit me?

    Lunelle Deluxe is designed with an adjustable velcro strap, so you can adapt it to your needs.

    The headphones are also easily adjustable for the perfect fit and an immersive audio experience.
    how do you charge it?

    Charging the Lunelle Deluxe is super easy! Unzip the top and connect the module with the included USB charger.

    Is there a "low battery" voice notification?

    No. Unlike cheaper alternatives, Lunelle will never wake you up at night with a "low battery" or "power off" voice prompt. The battery status can be seen in your Bluetooth settings.
    Can I wash the Lunelle Deluxe?

    Sure! Simply remove the electronics before washing and put them back in when dried. We recommend hand-washing it to make sure the material stays soft.
    what are the shipping times?

    Orders to the European Union take around 5-15 business days depending on the country of the final destination.
    Do you offer a warranty?

    At Lunelle we stand behind the quality of our products. Should you encounter a manufacturing defect, let us know within 1 Year of purchase and we will exchange your Lunelle for a new one, free of charge.
    is there a flashing light when it plays?

    No, Lunelle only flashes for a few seconds when switched on. As soon as it's paired with your device the light stops, so there's no annoying flashing light that keeps you or your partner awake at night!
    Do I need the Lunelle App to use my Lunelle?

    No. It works like normal Bluetooth headphones - use any app you like. The Lunelle App is optional and included free. If you want, you can still use Spotify, Calm, YouTube etc.
